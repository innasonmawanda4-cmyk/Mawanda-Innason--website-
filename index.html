Our Story: The Innason Legacy

A Tradition of Taste

Innason's was founded in 1998 by Chef Elias Innason with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that celebrated the freshest local ingredients with a touch of global inspiration. constantly seeking innovation in flavor and presentation.

For over two decades, we have been a cornerstone of the local community, dedicated to providing impeccable service and unforgettable cuisine.
